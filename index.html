OUR MISSION STATEMENT

At Dana Brothers Automotive & Diesel Service in Mesa, AZ, our number one goal is to provide a quality service at a fair price to all of our customers. We guarantee your satisfaction and work hard to earn your respect by providing outstanding customer service.

Our services

We work on all makes and models of cars and trucks. In addition to individuals, we work with small business owners and fleet managers to help them maintain their fleet of vehicles and help them keep their businesses running smoothly.

DieseL Specialist

Our staff is certified to work on Cummins, Duramax and Powerstroke Diesel Engines. We know diesel like nobody else and can fix problems other shops are not equipped to handle. Trucks or cars with diesel engines are our passion.

Free Shuttle Service Available

We take pride in going the extra mile for our valued customers by offering a complimentary shuttle service. Your comfort and convenience are our top priorities. While we work diligently to get your vehicle in top shape, our shuttle service ensures that your daily routine remains uninterrupted. Whether you're at work, home, or simply exploring the neighborhood, our shuttle will get you where you need to be.

Fleet Service Available

we understand that your business runs on wheels, and downtime can be costly. That's why we proudly offer free fleet services to our valued commercial customers. Our commitment goes beyond maintaining your vehicles; we aim to optimize your fleet's performance, lower operational costs, and minimize disruptions.

Loan Cars

We understand that life doesn't pause when your vehicle needs attention. That's why we offer a convenient loan car service to keep you moving forward. When you entrust your vehicle to us for repairs or maintenance, you can count on a complimentary loan car, ensuring you stay on schedule and maintain your independence. Our fleet of well-maintained loan cars provides a seamless transition .
